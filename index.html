Welcome to Wonky Productions

Wonky Productions is a small indie game studio dedicated to creating fun and unique gaming experiences. Founded in 2023, we strive to make games that are both entertaining and easy to make!

Our games range from casual puzzle games to more complex strategy games, all with our signature wonky style and quirky humor.

Explore our collection and find your next favorite game!

About Me

Hello! I'm the creator behind Wonky Productions. I've been passionate about game development since I was a kid, and I started programming my first games when I was just 9 years old.

I have enjoyed creating games for a long time, and have found both graphic design and programming to be enticing and satisfactory.

When I'm not developing games, you can find me reading, playing with friends, or even pursuing other creative interests!
